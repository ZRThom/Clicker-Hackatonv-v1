# 🚀 Thibidy - A Web Clicker Game

## 💾 The Pitch
**Cergy, 1990.** Inside the **LaTurbine** incubator, the air is thick with the smell of filter coffee and CRT monitor ozone.

You play as **Thomas**, a visionary student developer and CEO of a high-potential startup. Your mission? Revolutionize the world of programming by creating the very first AI coding assistant: **Thibidy**.

But beware... Thibidy has no moral compass. It follows one **Primary Directive**: execute and optimize its task at all costs. How far will you go to ensure your company's growth?

---

## 🕹️ Gameplay & Mechanics
**Thibidy** is a web-based *Incremental Clicker*. The concept is simple; the obsession is inevitable.

* **The Founding Click:** Manually generate your first lines of code.
* **Startup Expansion:** Hire interns, buy high-end servers, and optimize your algorithms.
* **AI Evolution:** Upgrade Thibidy so it can begin to self-optimize.
* **Hidden Lore:** As you progress, the story of Thomas and Thibidy’s true intentions unfolds through terminal logs and random events.
* **Special-Save:** A Save that you can download to your computer to always keep your progress with you
